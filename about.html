About Roshan Packaging

Established in 2000 by Mr. Roshan Shah, Roshan Packaging is a leading manufacturer, converter, and die-cut solutions provider based in Pune, Maharashtra, India. With over two decades of industry experience, we have built a strong presence across diverse sectors including solar, paper, packaging, printing, automotive, white goods, label, and wire harness industries.
At Roshan Packaging, we are committed to delivering high-performance adhesive tapes and die-cut solutions that meet the precise requirements of industrial applications. Our products are engineered for superior adhesion, durability, and consistency — ensuring optimal performance under demanding operational conditions.
Driven by quality, reliability, and continuous innovation, we employ advanced manufacturing processes and stringent quality control systems to meet global standards and customer expectations. Over the years, Roshan Packaging has become a trusted partner to businesses across India by offering customized solutions, timely delivery, and dedicated technical support. Our focus remains on building long-term relationships grounded in trust, performance, and value.

Our Mission, Vision And Value

Our Mission

At Roshan Packaging, our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable, and customized adhesive solutions that empower our customers to enhance their productivity and efficiency. We are dedicated to delivering consistent performance, timely service, and innovative products through advanced manufacturing practices — building long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and mutual growth.
