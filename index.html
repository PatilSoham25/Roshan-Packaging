About Us

Founded in 2000 by Mr. Roshan Shah, Roshan Packaging is a leading manufacturer and die-cut solutions provider based in Pune, India. With over 20 years of experience, we serve industries including solar, packaging, automotive, printing, and more.

We specialize in high-performance adhesive tapes and die-cut products designed for superior durability, adhesion, and reliability. Committed to quality and innovation, we deliver customized solutions with timely service and dedicated technical support.

Our Tape Collection

Packaging Tape

High-tack adhesive designed for heavy corrugated cartons, export packing, palletization, and long-haul logistics. Performs under dust, moisture, and temperature variations.

Double-Sided Tape

Structural bonding solution for fixing nameplates, trims, panels, gaskets, foam, and signage. Replaces rivets and screws, improves aesthetics, and distributes load evenly.

Insulation Tape

PVC and specialty insulation tapes for cable wrapping, wire harnessing, phase identification, moisture sealing, and electrical safety in low- to medium-voltage environments.

Protection Tape

Temporary surface shielding during machining, fabrication, painting, transport, and installation. Prevents scratches and abrasions while ensuring residue-free removal.

Safety Tape

Industrial marking system for lanes, pedestrian pathways, equipment boundaries, and hazard areas. Designed to withstand abrasion, forklifts, and high traffic.

Tesa Tape

Tesa is a German manufacturer of innovative adhesive tapes and solutions for bonding, insulation, protection, and industrial applications worldwide.
